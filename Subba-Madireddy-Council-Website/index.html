FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Can I vote in the Morrisville 2023 Election?

You can check your registration status on the State Board of Elections website.

How do I complete an application?

First, you can find which district you are located in. Then you can either complete an online application through the DMV or submit a mail-in application.

Can I come to Town Hall to meet with a Council Member?

Each Town Council Member manages his/her own schedule of availability. Please reach out to your Council representative directly if you wish to schedule an appointment to meet.
